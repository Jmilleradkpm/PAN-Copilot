FAQ

Common questions

Do I need my own Anthropic or OpenAI API key?
No. PAN Copilot handles the AI infrastructure. You just log in and start asking questions.
Is my config data stored or used for training?
Config text you paste is sent to the AI model for your query and is not stored beyond your conversation session. It is never used for model training.
Which PAN-OS versions does it know?
PAN-OS 8.x through 11.x, with version-aware answers — it knows which features exist in which release, and what changed between major versions.
Can it make mistakes?
Yes. It's an AI assistant, not a replacement for your own judgment. Treat answers like you would advice from a knowledgeable colleague — useful, but always verify before applying to production.
What's the difference between free and pro?
Free tier is 10 queries per day — enough to evaluate the tool. Pro is unlimited, for engineers who use it as part of their daily workflow.
Can I cancel Pro anytime?
Yes. Cancel from your billing portal anytime and you won't be charged again. You keep Pro access until the end of the billing period.
